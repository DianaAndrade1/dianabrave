Sailing Trip

I was not really one to take chances. Little did I know this trip this experience changed me in the best way possible. This broke me out of every single shell I had. It made great friendships and I kind of found myself. I seen beauty in the ocean and found beauty in the hardest place I never would imagine and that was myself.

Hope

Chicago Hope Academy was such a stressful dream at the age of 13. Hope is what I needed at the time and it was a Saturday when my Father got the mail. Recieving that big envelope was the reassurance that one needs sometimes that G-d is there when you need him. I continue to strive to thank him for the gift of the school that brought along many other priceless moments and gifts. And most of all the blessing that will always stick with me are the people he put in my life.

Asking for Help

My parents have been fighting a lot at home and it's been stressing me and my brother out. For a while I just kept it to myself, but it felt like I was bottling up my feelings about it. It was really hard to talk about with people, but I knew reaching out for help was better than ignoring my feelings.
